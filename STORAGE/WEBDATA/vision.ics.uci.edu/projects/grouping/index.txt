      Overview:
      
        

Consider the image patches at right. Though they lack global context, it is
often clear which contain object boundaries and which do not. We are developing
algorithms that integrate local image measurements in order to provide global
explanations of scenes in terms of objects, surfaces and their spatial
relations. This includes algorithms for bottom-up boundary detection and
segmentation from local features as well integrating top-down knowledge such as
the detection of familiar objects.
