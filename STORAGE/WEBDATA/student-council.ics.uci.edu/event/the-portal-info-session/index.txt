The Portal Info Session – “Coding for the Real World!”
Date: Tuesday, Feb 9th
Time: 6:30pm – 8:00pm
Location: DBH 6011
Come join us for free pizza and learn more about The Portal!
– Internship opportunities
– Technologies learned & Mentorship
– Our Clients & Accomplishments
– Q&A Panel
– Networking with The Portal Team
About The Portal:
The Portal believes in going beyond the academic setting and gaining knowledge through real world experiences. We are an exclusive place where students can develop their technical and entrepreneurial skills through experiential learning. If you are an ambitious student who has a hunger to learn and a passion for technology, we want you to join us.
We provide the following benefits:
– Paid internship program for web/mobile development, admin/management, and accounting.
– Learn to create professional web and mobile applications for real companies.
– Build valuable business and communication skills.
– Meet new people who are determined to fulfill their dreams.
– Work in a fun and casual start-up environment.
Graduates of our internship program are guaranteed to receive exciting job opportunities at start-ups and established companies, as well as business mentorship if the graduate desires to start their own company.
The Portal by K5 Ventures and The Beall Family Foundation
