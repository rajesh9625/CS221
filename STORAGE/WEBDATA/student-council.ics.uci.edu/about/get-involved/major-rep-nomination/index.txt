ICS Major Representative Program
ICS Major Representatives are elected officials of the ICSSC committees, in order to represent the voices of the students of their respective majors. They are here to establish a connection between the faculty and the students in order to solve possible comments, concerns, and complaints from both faculty and students in the school of ICS.
Responsibilities include delivering quarterly reports consisting of student concerns/needs for change relating to academic curriculum, academic/professional opportunities, or social events.
Requirements and Skills:
ICS Majors Only
This position is for the length of the academic year Fall-15 to end of Spring-16
Must be able to attend ICSSC Meetings as part of the AAA Committee, Monday’s from 4-5pm starting Week 6 @ ICS 432
Ability to take initiative and promote ICSSC to other ICS students
Ability to manage own work and deadlines
Ability to interact with a variety of individuals including other students, staff, and faculty
DEADLINE extended: Sunday, October 25th, 2015 at 4:00 PM
NOMINATIONS: https://student-council.ics.uci.edu/major-representative-nominations/
