   Captain Jean-Luc Picard (/ʒɑːnˌluːk pɨˌkɑrd/) is a fictional character who appears in fiction related to Star Trek. He appears in the television series Star Trek: The Next Generation and the feature films Star Trek Generations, Star Trek: First Contact,
    Star Trek: Insurrection and Star Trek: Nemesis. He also made a short appearance in the pilot episode of Star Trek: Deep Space Nine. He is portrayed by actor Patrick Stewart.
