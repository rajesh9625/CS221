The design of this facility is intended to enhance interaction between faculty and students and to create a progressive learning environment. Designed with flexibility in mind, the building accommodates the Bren School's growing faculty, staff and student populations. The first classes in Donald Bren Hall were held January 5th, 2007 and the Dean's office moved in on January 8th, 2007.
