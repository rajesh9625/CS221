  <div class="content clearfix">
    <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"><p>The Hana Ohana Research Group at the University of California, Irvine, studies a wide range of issues having to do with how people collaborate and what new technologies can do to help. We have a socio-technical perspective, looking at the relationship between the people involved and the tools they use. We focus on both discovering the underlying principles and guiding future practice to be more effective.</p>
