<h3>Summary Bio </h3>
<div class="content" style="width:1000px">
Siripen Pongpaichet is a first year Ph.D. student from the <a href="http://www.ics.uci.edu">Department of Computer Science</a>, at <a href="http://www.uci.edu">University of California, Irvine</a>. She works with Professor <a href="http://ngs.ics.uci.edu/">Ramesh Jain</a>, in the <a href="http://isg.ics.uci.edu">Information Systems Group</a>.  Her research interests include Event Information Systems, Large Scale Computation, and Database Management Systems. Currently, she is working on <a href="http://emme.ics.uci.edu/sln/">Social Life Networks</a> project which is the next generation of networks aiming to connect people to resources.  
<br />
<br />
She obtained her B.Sc in Computer Science with first class honors from <a href="http://www.mahidol.ac.th">Mahidol University</a>, Thailand in 2008. Her research project was E-Advisory System - an expert system providing academic advices liked a real human counselor. After graduating, she was a Lecturer and Software Engineering at the same university. She then came to United States in 2009, and obtained her M.S. in Computer Science from University of California, Irvine in 2011. She worked with Professor <a href="http://www.ics.uci.edu/~mjcarey/">Michael Carey</a> on her master thesis which is Monitoring Console for <a href="http://code.google.com/p/hyracks/">Hyracks Platform</a> - a real time web based console to monitor a data parallel platform running data-intensive jobs on a shared-noting cluster.
