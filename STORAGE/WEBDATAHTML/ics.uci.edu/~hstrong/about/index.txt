  <h1 class="page-title">About</h1>
  <p>My interests include statistics, machine learning, distributed systems, information visualization, and functional programming.</p>

<p>I hail from western Colorado. My undergraduate degree in Mathematics is from <a href="http://www.reed.edu/">Reed College</a> in Portland, Oregon. During college I studied abroad through the <a href="http://www.budapestsemesters.com/">Budapest Semesters in Mathematics</a> program. Between schools I worked in the private sector, notably co-founding a natural language analytics startup, <a href="http://luckysort.com/">Lucky Sort</a>.</p>
