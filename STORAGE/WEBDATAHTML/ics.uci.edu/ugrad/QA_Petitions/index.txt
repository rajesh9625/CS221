<td><a href="qa/ICS_UgradStudentPetition.pdf" target="_blank" title="ICS Undergraduate Student Petition">Undergraduate Student Petition</a></td>
<td>
<ul>
<li>Course substitution or waiver</li>
<li>Request credit for major or general education courses taken at a college for which there is no articulation agreement</li>
<li>Waive residence requirement</li>
<li>Request exception to an administrative decision, policy or deadline</li>
</ul>
<em>Note: Supporting documentation may be required</em></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><a href="https://ugradforms.ics.uci.edu/" target="_blank" title="Online ICS Prerequisite Clearing Request Form">Online Prerequisite Clearing Request</a></td>
<td>
<p>Course prerequisite(s) were satisfied via any of the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>AP exam credit</li>
<li>Transfer school credit for a course prerequisite</li>
<li>Access UCI credit for a course prerequisite</li>
<li>Course credit by student petition for a course prerequisite</li>
</ul>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>
<p>Part-Time Study</p>
<p>(paper form available from Registrar's Office or ICS Student Affairs)</p>
</td>
<td>
<p>If you meet one of these conditions:&#160;</p>
<ul>
<li>Work 20 or more hours a week*</li>
<li>Have health problems</li>
<li>Have significant family responsibilities</li>
</ul>
*Documentation required</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>
<p>Request Excess Units</p>
<p>(Download the <a href="qa/ICS_UgradStudentPetition.pdf" target="_blank" title="Undergraduate Student Petition">Undergraduate Student Petition</a>)</p>
</td>
<td>
<ul>
<li>Open to continuing students with the most recent quarterly and cumulative GPA of 3.0 or higher</li>
<li>Excess units are <em>not</em> intended to increase waitlist options. Only submit requests if you plan on taking more than four classes</li>
<li>Requests for over 20 units must detail which courses you intend to take and why</li>
<li>Petitions are subject to review and final approval by the Associate Dean</li>
</ul>
</td>
</tr>
