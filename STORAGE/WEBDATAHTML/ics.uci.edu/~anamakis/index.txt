<p align="justify"><span style="font-size:18.0pt;color:black">I am a 3<sup>rd</sup> year PhD 
candidate in Computer Science at the 
<a href="http://www.uci.edu/">University of California, Irvine</a>, working 
with <a href="http://www.ics.uci.edu/~dutt/">Professor Nikil Dutt</a> in </span>
<a href="http://duttgroup.ics.uci.edu/doku.php">DRG Lab</a>
<span style="font-size:18.0pt;color:black">on the
<a href="http://variability.org/">Variability Expedition</a>. </span>
I received my B.Sc. (Hons) and M.Sc. in Computer Engineering from
<a href="http://ece.ut.ac.ir/portal/">School of Electrical and Computer 
Engineering</a> at <a href="http://www.ut.ac.ir/en">University of Tehran </a>in 
2009 and 2012 respectively<span style="font-size:18.0pt">, where I worked with
<a href="http://navabi.com/NavabiResume2011-US.htm">Professor Zain Navabi</a> in
<a href="http://cad.ut.ac.ir/">CAD Research Group</a>. </span>My current 
research is in the area of &quot;Embedded Systems&quot; with a specific focus 
on &quot;Dynamic Variability and Reliability Management&quot;. Over the last years, I 
have 
researched in the areas of &quot;Power Management in VLSI System&quot; and &quot;Design for 
Testability&quot;. My CV can be found <a href="CV-MajidShoushtari.pdf">here</a>.</p>
