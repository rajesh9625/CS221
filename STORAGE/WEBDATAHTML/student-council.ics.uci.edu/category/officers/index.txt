<p>My name is Brandon Hastings and I am excited to serve as your 2015-16 ICS Student Council President. I&#8217;m a 4th year majoring in Business Information Management.</p>
<p>As President, my goal is to enrich the <a href="http://www.ics.uci.edu/ugrad/resources/index" target="_blank">ICS Student Life</a> &#8212; to be a memorable and rewarding experience of immerse opportunity, personal development and leadership.</p>
<p>This cannot be done alone, we need your help &#8212; so let&#8217;s make a difference, and join the ICS Student Council!</p>
