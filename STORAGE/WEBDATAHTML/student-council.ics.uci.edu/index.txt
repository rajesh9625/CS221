        <h2 class="entry-title"><a href="https://student-council.ics.uci.edu/icssc-committee-meetings-schedule/">ICSSC Committee Meetings &#8211; Schedule</a></h2>

        <span class="vcard" style="display: none;"><span class="fn"><a href="https://student-council.ics.uci.edu/author/bhasting/" title="Posts by Brandon Hastings" rel="author">Brandon Hastings</a></span></span><span class="updated" style="display:none">2016-02-07T19:39:29+00:00</span><div class="entry-content"><h3 style="text-align: left;">ICSSC Committee Meeting times have been posted on the ICS Calendar <a href="https://student-council.ics.uci.edu/events/category/icssc/list/">here</a>.</h3>
<p>Committee Meetings are weekly from Week 2 &#8211; 9 for the Winter Quarter.</p>
<p>ICS Students interested in joining a committee as a member or mentor are encouraged to come and visit us at any of our meetings. Information on how to join Council&#8217;s official roster will be posted on our websiteÂ this week.</p>
<h2>Mac Lab Information</h2>
<p>The lab is located in ICS2, Room 110.</p>
<p>Access to the Kay Family Foundation Innovation Lab will be granted on a quarterly basis to students in a Bren School of ICS major. Students must request access from the Bren School facilities viaÂ <a href="http://keys.ics.uci.edu/">keys.ics.uci.edu</a>, specifying Mary Stasik as Supervisor. <strong>Access to the lab is <span class="aBn" tabindex="0" data-term="goog_1927410726"><span class="aQJ">7am to 10pm</span></span> daily.</strong> Access will expire at the end of each quarter.</p>
<p><strong>Important Guidelines:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Students are not to open the door for unauthorized users, at the risk of losing room privileges for a full quarter. Students are not to prop the door open, at the risk of losing room privileges permanently.</li>
<li>Food or drinks (including water) are not allowed in the lab, and anybody found with food/drink in the room will lose room privileges for a full quarter.</li>
</ul>
<p>The lab will be monitored via periodic checks by ICS Student Council members who helped design the lab and develop these policies. The ICS Student Council has been tasked with supporting the Bren School in its enforcement of these rules.</p>
<p>More informationÂ aboutÂ the Mac Lab is availableÂ <a href="https://www.ics.uci.edu/about/kfflab/">here</a>.</p>

        <h2 class="entry-title"><a href="https://student-council.ics.uci.edu/icssc-week-1-general-meeting/">ICSSC Week 1 General Meeting</a></h2>

        <span class="vcard" style="display: none;"><span class="fn"><a href="https://student-council.ics.uci.edu/author/bhasting/" title="Posts by Brandon Hastings" rel="author">Brandon Hastings</a></span></span><span class="updated" style="display:none">2016-02-07T19:42:37+00:00</span><div class="entry-content"><p>Invite your ICS friends!! TONIGHT at 7pm Room DBH 6011. All ICS students are eligible General Members and may attend General Meetings that are every other odd week this quarter. Want to get more involved? Become a member of one of our four Committees: Corporate Outreach, Student Events, Faculty Relations (known as AAA), or join our Project Management Committee and learn a new language with a like minded team!</p>
<p>//ICSSC Committee Membership signup forms will be released during our meeting today and be available on our website (if you were unable to make today&#8217;s meeting). If you class schedule conflicts with general meetings, you can still get involved and join one of the Committees, which have their own weekly meeting starting Week 2. A schedule of all the committees meeting times will be made available this week on our website.</p>
<p>ICSSC is what you make of it. We facilitate the tools and many opportunities for ICS students to achieve their goals as ICS majors. Whether it be finding a summer internship, networking with professionals, making friends, or developing the next best app&#8212;ICSSC alumni leave saying that ICSSC was one of the most rewarding and memorable experiences they had at UCI.</p>
<p>RSVP &amp; More Details: <a href="https://www.facebook.com/events/1112699308741865/" target="_blank">https://www.facebook.com/events/1112699308741865/</a></p>
