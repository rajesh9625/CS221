<p class="small-heading"><b>Data Set Information:</b></p>
<p class="normal">This file concerns credit card applications.  All attribute names and values have been changed to meaningless symbols to protect confidentiality of the data.<br>  <br>This dataset is interesting because there is a good mix of attributes -- continuous, nominal with small numbers of values, and nominal with larger numbers of values.  There are also a few missing values.</p>

<br />

<p class="small-heading"><b>Attribute Information:</b></p>
<p class="normal">A1:	b, a.<br>A2:	continuous.<br>A3:	continuous.<br>A4:	u, y, l, t.<br>A5:	g, p, gg.<br>A6:	c, d, cc, i, j, k, m, r, q, w, x, e, aa, ff.<br>A7:	v, h, bb, j, n, z, dd, ff, o.<br>A8:	continuous.<br>A9:	t, f.<br>A10:	t, f.<br>A11:	continuous.<br>A12:	t, f.<br>A13:	g, p, s.<br>A14:	continuous.<br>A15:	continuous.<br>A16: +,-         (class attribute)</p>
