<p class="small-heading"><b>Data Set Information:</b></p>
<p class="normal"> --- By using a tweet crawler, we collect 2000 labelled tweets (1000 positive tweets and 1000 negative ones)<br>       on various topics such as: politics and arts. These tweets include opinions written in both<br>       Modern Standard Arabic (MSA) and the Jordanian dialect.<br><br>   --- The selected tweets convey some kind of feelings (positive or negative) and the objective of our model is<br>        to extract valuable information from such tweets in order to determine the sentiment orientation of the inputted text.<br>       The months-long annotation process of the tweets is manually conducted mainly by two human experts<br>       (native speakers of Arabic). If both experts agree on the label of a certain tweet, then the tweet is assigned this label.<br>       Otherwise, a third expert is consulted to break the tie. <br><br>   --- Predicted attribute: class of opinion polarity.</p>

<br />
