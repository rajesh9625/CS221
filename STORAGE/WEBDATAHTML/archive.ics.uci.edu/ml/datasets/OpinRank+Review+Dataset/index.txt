<p class="small-heading"><b>Data Set Information:</b></p>
<p class="normal">Car Reviews<br>------------<br>-Full reviews of cars for model-years 2007, 2008, and 2009<br>-There are about 140-250 cars for each model year<br>-Extracted fields include dates, author names, favorites and the full textual review<br>-Total number of reviews: ~42,230<br><br>Hotel Reviews<br>--------------<br>-Full reviews of hotels in 10 different cities (Dubai, Beijing, London, New York City, New Delhi, San Francisco, Shanghai, Montreal, Las Vegas, Chicago)<br>-There are about 80-700 hotels in each city<br>-Extracted fields include date, review title and the full review<br>-Total number of reviews: ~259,000</p>
