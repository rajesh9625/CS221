<p class="small-heading"><b>Data Set Information:</b></p>
<p class="normal">People used for recording of the data were wearing four tags (ankle left, ankle right, belt and chest). <br>Each instance is a localization data for one of the tags. The tag can be identified by one of the attributes.</p>

<br />

<p class="small-heading"><b>Attribute Information:</b></p>
<p class="normal">Instance example: A01,020-000-033-111,633790226057226795,27.05.2009 14:03:25:723,4.292500972747803,2.0738532543182373,1.36650812625885,walking<br><br>   1) Sequence Name {A01,A02,A03,A04,A05,B01,B02,B03,B04,B05,C01,C02,C03,C04,C05,D01,D02,D03,D04,D05,E01,E02,E03,E04,E05} (Nominal)<br>      - A, B, C, D, E  = 5 people<br>   2) Tag identificator {010-000-024-033,020-000-033-111,020-000-032-221,010-000-030-096}	(Nominal)<br>      - ANKLE_LEFT = 010-000-024-033<br>      - ANKLE_RIGHT = 010-000-030-096<br>      - CHEST = 020-000-033-111<br>      - BELT = 020-000-032-221<br>   3) timestamp (Numeric) all unique<br>   4) date FORMAT = dd.MM.yyyy HH:mm:ss:SSS (Date) <br>   5) x coordinate of the tag (Numeric)<br>   6) y coordinate of the tag (Numeric)<br>   7) z coordinate of the tag (Numeric)<br>   8) activity  {walking,falling,'lying down',lying,'sitting down',sitting,'standing up from lying','on all fours','sitting on the ground','standing up from sitting','standing up from sitting on the ground'} (Nominal)<br>  </p>
